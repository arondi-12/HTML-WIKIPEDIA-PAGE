Volkswagen Golf Mk7

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

TheVOLKSWAGEN GOLF (Mk7)is a C-segment car manufactured by German automobile manufacturer Volkswagen. It's the seventh generation in the Golf series and the successor to the Golf (MK6). It has a 2.0L 4Cylinder Turbo charged engine that boasts 306HP and a whooping 383Nm of torque. It has a DSG gearbox that shifts rapidly enhancing it's performance.
